About Horizone

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Horizone began with a simple mission: to make premium car rental accessible to everyone. What started as a small fleet of carefully selected vehicles has grown into one of the most trusted names in the car rental industry.

Over the years, we've expanded our services, enhanced our fleet, and built lasting relationships with thousands of satisfied customers. Our commitment to quality, safety, and exceptional customer service remains at the heart of everything we do.
